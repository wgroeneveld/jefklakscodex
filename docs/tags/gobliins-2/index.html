Gobliins 2: the Review

  10/2006

Goblin Princes are Ugly Am I the only one wondering what the heck the lead artist ate the day he created Goblins? Uh oh, did I spell this wrong? Not really. In Gobliiins, the prequel, you can control three Goblin creatures, hence the three ‘i’-s. This game comes with two wacky dudes and the sequel with only one. Every year an article pops up about Adventure games and the death of the genre and every year a fantastic game gets released to counter this myth.
